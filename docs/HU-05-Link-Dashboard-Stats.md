# HU-05: Link Dashboard & Stats

- Epic: Epic 3 - Analytics & Management
- Story: As an Active User, I want to see a list of my links ordered by date and their click counts, so that I know which ones are performing best.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. The list must show the top 100 most frequent URLs `[Source 260]`.
2. The list allows differentiation between unique visits vs. recurrent visits `[Source 259]`.
